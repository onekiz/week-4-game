Cyrstal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four cyrstals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of the crystals.
